Fish and Chips

Fish and chips is a classic British dish consisting of battered fish and deep-fried chips. It's crispy, delicious, and perfect for a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thick chips
  • 2 fillets of white fish (e.g., cod or haddock)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup cold sparkling water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Method

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Blanch the chips in the hot oil for 3-4 minutes, then remove and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Gradually whisk in the sparkling water to create a smooth batter.
  4. Season the fish fillets with salt and pepper, then dip them into the batter, ensuring they are fully coated.
  5. Carefully place the battered fish into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. Return the chips to the hot oil and fry until golden and crispy, about 2-3 minutes. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve the fish and chips hot with your favorite condiments, such as tartar sauce or malt vinegar.

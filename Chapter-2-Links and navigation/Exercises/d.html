Alice in Borderland

Alice in Borderland is a Japanese science fiction thriller drama based on the manga of the same name. The story follows Arisu and his friends as they are transported to a parallel world where they must compete in deadly games to survive.

Key Information

  • Genre: Science Fiction, Thriller
  • Original Release: December 2020
  • Streaming Platform: Netflix

Cast

  • Kento Yamazaki as Ryohei Arisu
  • Tao Tsuchiya as Yuzuha Usagi
  • Keita Machida as Daikichi Karube

Characters

The series features a variety of characters, each with unique skills and personalities that contribute to their survival in the games.

Reviews

Alice in Borderland has been praised for its intense storytelling, gripping visuals, and strong performances by the cast.
